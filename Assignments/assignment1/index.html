Summary

Herald is a video game developed in the Unreal 3D Game Engine in which the player is The Captain of an interstellar spaceship. The Captain must livestream their gameplay on the Twitch social game streaming platform so that the Twitch viewers can join as The Crew of the spaceship, performing actions by typing in the Twitch IRC chatroom. Together, the The Captain and Crew can scan, shoot weapons at, beam away teams to and comandeer enemy spaceships and space stations, as well as beam to the surface of planets to build cities.

Made For Twitch

The Twitch social game streaming platform is a service through which a player can livestream their gameplay so that many other people can watch the gameplay in realtime and comment on it in an adjoing text-based IRC chatroom. Twitch is optimized to allow hundreds of simultaneous viewers to watch the video in near-realtime. Herald is a game designed with this in mind, showing many character's information on screen simultaneously in the spaceship's Room System. Additionally, Herald saves both the streamer's spaceship and each of the active viewer's characters betweem sessions, as well as other information about the game world like cities and enemy resources, making it a persistent shared experience.

IRC CoopRPG

The text-based IRC chatroom adjoining each Twitch stream is an excellent choice for lightweight authentication of players, since each user in the IRC chatroom is unique and logged in through the Twitch API. With this in mind, a user can issue commands to their character in the game by typing them in to the IRC chatroom, preceeded by the '!!' delimiter. The streamer's running game reads the IRC chat messages and forwards them to the appropriate game object. In Herald, the viewer's characters are represented as a name in the ship's Room System (or in the Away Team of Fighter Systems), with their stats and selected targets changing in response to the viewer's text actions. The cooperative nature of the game is expected to encourage interaction between viewers of the stream, as well as create a unique story on each session.
